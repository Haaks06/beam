How it works

Three steps, no learning curve

Beam is one button, not a settings menu. Pair, send, and let the countdown clock do the cleanup for you.

01

Pair

Open Beam on both devices. Scan a QR code or type a 6-character pairing code — no login, ever.

02

Send

Drop a photo, paste a link, or type a note. It's on the wire and landing on the other device before you can second-guess it.

03

Forgotten

Your timed window closes (5 minutes by default, adjustable from Settings) and the relay deletes both devices, every item, every upload. Pair again next time you need it.

Ephemeral by design

Built to forget you

Most tools want to remember everything about you, forever. Beam does the opposite. Two devices pair, get exactly two minutes to send and receive, and then the relay deletes every device, every item, and every uploaded file it was holding — no persistent link between your phone and your PC survives the session.

  • No accounts to create or passwords to remember
  • Capped at exactly two devices per pairing
  • Nothing left behind for the relay to leak

Real internet, not just Wi-Fi

Not AirDrop with extra steps

AirDrop-style tools only work when both devices share the same Wi-Fi network. Beam relays over the real internet, so your phone on cellular data can reach your PC across the house, across town, or across a conference room you're not even connected to.

Wi-Fi, cellular, different networks entirely — if both devices can reach the internet, Beam works.

Private by design

Private by design, not by promise

Every device is authenticated with a long random token scoped to its own pairing — a stranger can't read your items, drop into your pairing, or make it a threesome. The source is public, so you don't have to take our word for any of this.
